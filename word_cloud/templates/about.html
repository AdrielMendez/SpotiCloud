{{ name }}

{{domain}} is a project created to allow users to create word clouds out of their spotify information. We decided to take advantage of most of the parameters provided to us and create a custom form for many options. We're music lovers who love to express our taste with our friends, so we decided to create this application after observing how large the quantity of data given by the Spotify API is. This also was an experimentation for us to experience deploying our own application to the public. We hope to continue to update this website to allow for more future customizations in hopes that people will enjoy using it.

{{domain}} is not affiliated With Spotify or Genius.
